Calling All Vigo Skateboarders!

We want you!

Women Who Skateboard · Vigo is a group of like-minded individuals. A bunch of gals who just to have fun, skateboarding around the city together. Founded in 2023, we organise meet-ups in different parts of the city where we can all skate together, help each other out, learn some new tricks and skills and hopefully make some new friends along the way.

We emphasise inclusion and fun and most respect. When you join a Women Who Skateboard · Vigo meet-up you are guaranteed the chance to have some fun on your board, and feel safe, secure and supported in your surroundings. For now we're focused on skateboarding but we are open to expanding our meet-ups to include all other board sports on land, on water or on snow. So watch this space and more importantly, come and join in!
